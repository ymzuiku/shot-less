**完整描述（4000字符以内）**
```
Tired of scrolling through hundreds of screenshots cluttering your camera roll? Delete Screenshots makes it effortless to find, review, and bulk delete every screenshot on your device.

HOW IT WORKS
Delete Screenshots scans your entire photo library and groups all screenshots by date, so you can see exactly when you took them. Browse through them at a glance in a beautiful grid view, then select and delete the ones you don't need anymore.

KEY FEATURES

Smart Screenshot Detection
Automatically finds every screenshot in your photo library — no manual searching needed. Screenshots are grouped by date, making it easy to identify which ones came from the same session.

Batch Selection & Deletion
Select multiple screenshots at once and delete them all with a single tap. A confirmation prompt keeps you safe from accidental deletions.

Auto Select
In a hurry? Use Auto Select to rapidly highlight screenshots in sequence. Pause anytime to review before deleting. Perfect for clearing out hundreds of screenshots in seconds.

Pinch to Resize Grid
Customize your browsing experience by pinching to resize the grid from 1 to 6 columns. See more at once, or zoom in for a closer look.

Full-Screen Preview
Tap any screenshot to view it full screen. Swipe left and right to browse through screenshots, pinch to zoom up to 5x, and swipe down to dismiss.

Privacy First
All processing happens entirely on your device. Delete Screenshots never uploads your photos anywhere — your images stay private.

PERFECT FOR
• Clearing screenshots after reading articles, saving addresses, or capturing receipts
• Freeing up storage space on your iPhone or iPad
• Organizing your photo library before backing up
• Anyone who takes lots of screenshots and wants a cleaner camera roll

Delete Screenshots is the fastest, simplest way to take control of your screenshot collection. Download it free today and reclaim your camera roll.
```
