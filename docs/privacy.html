Privacy Policy

Clean Screenshots (清理截图)

Effective date: March 31, 2026

Thank you for using Clean Screenshots. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or data from your device. Clean Screenshots does not have servers, does not require an account, and does not connect to the internet.

2. Photo Library Access

Clean Screenshots requires access to your photo library solely to display and delete screenshots on your device. All processing happens entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any third party — including us.

3. Data Storage

The app does not store any user data outside of your device. No analytics, crash reports, or usage data are collected.

4. Third-Party Services

Clean Screenshots does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because we collect no data at all, the app is safe for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: github.com/ymzuiku/shot-less/issues
